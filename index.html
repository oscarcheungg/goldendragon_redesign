Where we started

OUR STORY

For over 30 years, Golden Dragon has been a beloved part of the community—a place where tradition meets fresh flavor in every bite. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a go-to spot for Chinese cuisine, blending generations-old recipes with a modern twist.

We're passionate about honoring Chinese culinary heritage while bringing new excitement to the table. Every dish is made with care and the freshest ingredients—because to us, great food is all about quality, culture, and sharing something special.
